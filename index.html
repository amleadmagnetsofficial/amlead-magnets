Who We Are

Hype Air Net is an independent third-party service assistance platform founded in 2026. We are not a cable television provider, internet service provider, or streaming service. We operate independently from all service providers and receive no commission or payment from them. Our sole purpose is to guide and assist customers in understanding their options.

What We Do

We provide unbiased guidance and assistance for customers exploring cable, internet, and streaming services. Our specialists help you understand available options, coverage areas, features, and pricing so you can make informed decisions. We then facilitate your connection with the appropriate service provider for account setup.

What Makes Us Different

  • True Independence: No affiliations with service providers means unbiased guidance based on your needs, not partnerships.
  • Complete Transparency: Service fees are separate, clearly disclosed, and never hidden in provider billing.
  • Your Privacy Matters: We never request passwords, account numbers, or sensitive credentials. Your data is protected and never shared without consent.
  • 24/7 Support: Real people answering phones, not automated systems. We're here when you need us.
  • Refund Guarantee: Refund requests within 7-15 days of service purchase, subject to eligibility criteria.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing honest, transparent, and helpful guidance. We never claim to represent service providers, never impersonate official representatives, and never mislead customers about our role. Our success is measured by your satisfaction with our assistance, not by how much you spend with a provider.
